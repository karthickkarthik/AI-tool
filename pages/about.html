About AI Tools

We're on a mission to democratize artificial intelligence and make powerful AI tools accessible to businesses of all sizes.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of AI researchers and entrepreneurs, AI Tools was born from a simple belief: artificial intelligence should be accessible to everyone, not just large corporations with massive budgets.

What started as a small team working out of a garage has grown into a leading platform serving over 50,000 businesses worldwide. We've helped companies across industries—from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises—transform their operations with the power of AI.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, customer-centric development, and an unwavering commitment to making AI tools that actually work for real-world business challenges.
