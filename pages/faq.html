Pricing & Plans

What's included in each pricing plan?

Starter ($29/month): 5 AI tools, 1,000 API calls, email support, basic analytics, 5GB storage.
Professional ($99/month): All AI tools, 10,000 API calls, priority support, advanced analytics, 50GB storage, team collaboration.
Enterprise ($299/month): All features, unlimited API calls, 24/7 phone support, custom analytics, unlimited storage, dedicated manager.

Can I change my plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences for the current month.

Yes! When you choose annual billing, you save 20% compared to monthly billing. This discount is automatically applied at checkout when you select the yearly option.

If you exceed your monthly API limits, we'll notify you and offer options to upgrade your plan or purchase additional API calls. You won't lose access to your account, and we'll work with you to find the best solution.
